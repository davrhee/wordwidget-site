Privacy

WordWidget collects no data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Everything the app does happens on your device. Your word history, quiz results, and settings never leave your phone, and the app never connects to the internet.

Since nothing is collected, there is nothing to sell, share, lose, or breach.

This policy applies to the WordWidget iOS app and this website. The website is a static page and sets no cookies. If anything here changes, this page will say so plainly.
